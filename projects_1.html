Smart Mask

I led a multidisciplinary team that consisted of members from both ECE and MBE from design to a final prototype.

This project consisted of a mask that can log data from a temperature sensor and an SpO2 sensor. This data is then fed to both SD card and a Bluetooth device such as a smartphone.
